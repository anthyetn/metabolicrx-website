Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions do you treat?
We specialize in three key areas of men's health: testosterone replacement therapy (TRT) for low testosterone, erectile dysfunction treatments, and hair loss prevention and restoration. Each treatment is personalized to your specific needs.
How does the telehealth process work?
Simply complete a brief medical intake form, then consult with a licensed healthcare provider via video or phone. If treatment is appropriate, your prescription is sent to a pharmacy and delivered directly to your home. Follow-up consultations ensure your treatment stays on track.
Is testosterone therapy safe?
When supervised by qualified healthcare providers, TRT is generally safe for most men. Your Metabolic Rx provider will monitor your progress through regular follow-ups and lab work to ensure treatment remains safe and effective.
How quickly will I see results?
Results vary by treatment. For testosterone therapy, most men notice improvements in energy and mood within 3-6 weeks. ED medications work within hours. Hair loss treatments typically show visible results within 3-6 months of consistent use.
How much does treatment cost?
Metabolic Rx offers transparent, affordable pricing with no insurance required. Your provider will discuss specific costs during your consultation based on your personalized treatment plan.
Are the treatments FDA-approved?
Yes, we only prescribe FDA-approved medications including testosterone, sildenafil, tadalafil, finasteride, and minoxidil. All treatments are dispensed by licensed pharmacies.
